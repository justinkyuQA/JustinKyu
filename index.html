About

Welcome. I'm Justin Kyu, an independent developer focused on creating small, practical educational tools that help people understand complex technical concepts through experimentation. Greenfield Projects is my long-term effort to build an ecosystem of open-source learning software covering cybersecurity, Python, AI, networking, Linux, and technical research.

My Journey

My development journey has been centered around consistent progress. Every day I focus on building, testing, documenting, publishing, and improving software. Instead of waiting for perfection, I believe in releasing working software, learning from experience, and steadily expanding the Greenfield ecosystem.
